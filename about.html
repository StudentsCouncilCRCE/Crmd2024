About Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues

College of Engineering

Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering (FR.CRCE) is a top private Autonomous engineering institution in the heart of Mumbai. The college emphasizes on innovation and comprehensive education, focusing on the latest technological trends while fostering a holistic learning environment. With the implementation of NEP 2020, FR.CRCE will create its own curriculum from this year. It has earned an "A" grade from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). Its Computer Engineering department is also accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).

About The Student's Council

The Student Council at Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering (Fr. CRCE) is the hub of student activity on campus. With a team of dedicated representatives, the council organizes events, facilitates communication with the administration, and fosters a strong sense of community. By hosting a variety of activities, from workshops to festivals, the Student Council creates a vibrant and inclusive college experience, playing a key role in shaping the culture at Fr. CRCE.

About Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues
Memorial Debate

The Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues Memorial Debate is a renowned annual event in Mumbai, organized by Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering (FR.CRCE), Bandra. This prestigious debate competition brings together talented students from across the country, creating a vibrant platform for engaging in thoughtful and rigorous discussions. The 26th edition of the event, hosted by the Students' Council of FR.CRCE, will take place on the 23rd and 24th of August 2024.
As one of Mumbai's most respected debate competitions, the Memorial Debate provides an opportunity for young people to articulate their views on a variety of contemporary issues. It is a showcase for the power of rational thinking, logical argumentation, and compelling evidence in debating real world topics. This event underscores the college's dedication to fostering intellectual growth and highlights the value of structured debate in promoting critical thinking and civil discourse among the next generation of leaders.

About Akrasia

About Akrasia

The theme "Akrasia: The Moral Maze of Conflict and Coalition" explores the intricate dynamics of acting against one's better judgment, as encapsulated by the Greek term "akrasia." Participants will delve into the complexities of human behavior, examining how ethical principles clash with personal desires and the challenges of forming alliances in the face of adversity. This theme invites debaters to navigate the tensions between right and wrong, the nature of internal and external conflicts, and the strategies involved in building and maintaining coalitions despite diverse moral views.
The theme also extends to real-life issues such as politics, where ethical compromises and conflicting interests are commonplace, and other areas of life where individuals and groups must balance moral integrity with practical decision-making. Through this exploration, the competition aims to foster critical thinking and robust discussion on the nuanced interplay between ethics, conflict, and cooperation.
